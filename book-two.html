Maxi And Helium 2

The Hunt For the Golden Cheese

A book for the whole family! Mainly aimed at children between 6 and 10 years, but is also excellent as a reading book for children from 4 years. Are you ready? Only the two little mice Mickey and Helium can save the Animal Kingdom and its inhabitants from the evil Pigeon Hunters. It all depends on them finding the Golden Cheese. In the exciting hunt, the mice meet both good and evil creatures. Who they can trust is not easy to know. It turns out that their friends Ta-Ta and Aunt Gamelina have not told the whole truth about the Animal Kingdom.The secrets and surprises are many. Mysterious things happen that cannot be explained - invisible bridges appear, creatures go up in smoke and stumps seem to be able to talk. It will not be a calm start to the adventure for our little friends. But most important of all ... now they're on their way. Come along!
