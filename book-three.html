Maxi And Helium 3

The adventure in Lindrizia

A book for the whole family! Mainly aimed at children between 6 and 10 years, but is also excellent as a reading book for children from 4 years. The hunt for Guldosten continues ... Now you get to follow the little mice into another magical world - the winter land of Lindrizia, which is Princess Frah's beautiful kingdom. But there also lives the terrible snow monster Galaha. If he gets hold of Mickey and Helium, their adventure is over! The mice not only face nasty dangers on their way, but also get to see and experience amazing things. For example, invisible waiters, a three-headed giraffe and flying dogs!
Will Mickey and Helium find the clues that lead to their parents and the Golden Cheese? Even if everything does not go as planned, one thing is certain: Mickey and Helium are very brave. They are really doing their best to save the Animal Kingdom and all its inhabitants from the evil Dove Hunters.
