Maxi And Helium 1

The Mystery of the Hole in the Wall

A book for the whole family! Mainly aimed at children between 6 and 10 years, but is also excellent as a reading book for children from 4 years. Let the adventure begin ... The two little mice Mickey and Helium have mysteriously ended up at the home of a girl named Camilla. When the mice one day discover a hole in the wall and are sucked into it, an exciting and magical adventure begins! They land in a world completely different from spring called the Animal Kingdom. It turns out that Mickey and Helium are princes and princesses in the Animal Kingdom and therefore they are given a dangerous but very important mission. With the help of newfound friends, they get ready to be sent on the adventure of a lifetime. They must find the lost Golden Cheese and secure the Animal Kingdom from the evil Dove Hunters and all evil.
