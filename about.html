  • Camilla Brinck Biography

    Camilla Brinck was born on the 24/may/1974 and raised outside Stockholm. Choosing a profession with a creative focus was always a matter of course for Camilla. In addition to writing about Maxi and Helium, she has also worked as a popartist, songwriter, producer, and DJ, She is known for the singles "Bye Bye Forever (Chiki Chiki)", "Heaven" "Tell Me" and .To be able to create for children, Camilla thinks is one of the best things you can do. She looks forward to writing more books and audiobooks adventures that as many children as possible will be able to enjoy.
